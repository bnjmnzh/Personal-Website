Hi, I'm Benjamin Zhuo. I am a fourth-year computer science and statistics student studying at the University of Toronto.

Thus, I was led to taking lots of math courses as well as my computer science courses at UofT. I really enjoyed my intro to combinatorics class.

In my spare time I like to hit the gym and go for the very-occasional swim. I also play some video games. You can usually find me playing Teamfight Tactics or Osu! and sometimes Apex Legends.
